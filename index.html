Why TrueMath

AI got you to the door. TrueMath gets you through.

AI changed how you and your team work. You're more productive, you sound and look more professional. The next phase is the numbers. Imagine what happens to dealflow, closes, and client engagement when the math gets the same upgrade.

Ask the way you actually talk.
No formulas, no spreadsheet syntax. Describe the calculation the way you'd explain it to a colleague. TrueMath figures out the rest and returns a result you can stake your reputation on.
Every answer is auditable.
TrueMath doesn't estimate. It finds the right calculation path, runs the math precisely, and returns the result with full lineage: variables used, steps taken, logic behind every number.
Shareable proof, not just an answer.
Speak the calculation into existence as fast as the conversation moves. Send it to a client with a quote. Share it with a counterparty on a deal. Everyone is looking at the same math, the same method, the same assumptions, so the room can agree and move on.
Teamwork powered by reliable math.
TrueMath gives your team a focal point. One set of calculations, one set of defaults, one set of customizations. The most powerful tool you can take into battle is coordination.

About TrueMath

Built on 30 years of calculation experience and 30 million users

Every time technology advances, our tools for working with numbers evolve too — from abacuses to calculators to spreadsheets, and now to AI.

TrueMath is built on PowerOne, a calculation platform developed over 30 years by TrueMath co-founder Elia Freedman. With 30 million downloads and a reputation for precision across finance, engineering, and science, PowerOne became the foundation Elia committed to TrueMath — bringing three decades of real-world calculation experience into the AI age.

The result is a math engine with thirty years of real-world use behind it, rebuilt for the way professionals work today.
