The problem

Three ways calculation features quietly kill your roadmap.

Problem 01
LLMs hallucinate numbers
GPT gets the vibe right. It gets the math wrong. One wrong loan estimate, one incorrect tax figure, one hallucinated return projection, and you own the support ticket.
Problem 02
Hardcoded formulas rot
You ship a working formula. Six months later a product requirement changes, a regulation updates, or a client has a new edge case. Now it's a three-sprint refactor.
Problem 03
No audit trail, no enterprise deal
Your first enterprise prospect asks how you handle calculation traceability. "It's in the code" is not the answer they want. Deals stall. Procurement loops. Quarters slip.
